Communication and Office Hours

Office Hours

I am a part-time faculty member and do not have an office on campus, but I can meet in person after my last CSC110 class ends at 3:15 on Tuesday/Thursday. You can meet me in CCE 030 between 3:15 and 3:45, and if we need to stay later then we can move to the modular unit on the second floor of CCE.

I normally end office hours once all students have left. Please send me an email ahead of time if you plan to arrive after 3:30; otherwise, I may not still be there when you show up.

We can also schedule a remote meeting on a different day if my in-person time doesn't work for you. If you'd like to meet remotely, then please send me an email with your availability over the next 2-3 days. I'll try to find a time that works for both of us and schedule a Zoom meeting.

Blackboard

I will post or link to all course material and assignments on Blackboard. If you want to find something or submit an assignment, go to our Blackboard site and look in the appropriate page. If you think I forgot to post something, then send me an email and I'll either post it or tell you where to find it.

Email

My email is zckohlberg@qu.edu. I check my email most weekdays and try to respond to all emails within 24 hours. If you email me after Friday morning or on the weekend, then I probably won't see it until Monday. You're still welcome to email me at any time.

I will answer most questions via email if you don't want to ask in person. If you're asking for help with an assignment, coding issue, or software, then please attach your code (the text file, not a screenshot). Try to include as much relevant information as possible and be specific about what you want to know. This helps me give you a useful answer more quickly.

If you want me to do something more involved than answer a few questions, such as review your code or help you debug a program, then you should meet with me during office hours. I'm happy to help you with coding, debugging, and other tasks as long as you're the one doing the task. You should not come to office hours or send an email expecting me to do your work for you.
